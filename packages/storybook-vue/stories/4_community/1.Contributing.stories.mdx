# Contributing to Scale

Scale enables the developer community of Telekom to share their skills: Wether it's a bug you fix, improved usability or entire new components: Contribution is what makes Scale an invaluable asset to Telekoms Product Ecosystem.

## How you can contribute to scale:

- Report an issue in our repository
- Fix a bug and send a pull request
- Ask a question in our Slack Channel
- If you're seeking to contribute design work and new components, Send an e-mail to Dennys.Hess@telekom.com
